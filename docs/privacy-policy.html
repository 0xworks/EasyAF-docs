1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how EasyAF ("the App") handles information when you use the Android application.

EasyAF is a local-first app designed to help users manage project records and related documents. The App does not require account registration and does not provide a cloud backend for storing your project data.

2. Information Processed by the App

EasyAF allows users to create and store content directly on their device, including:

  • project details
  • site diary entries
  • purchase orders
  • incident reports
  • photo attachments
  • signatures
  • app settings and other content you choose to save in the App

This information is stored locally on your device.

3. How Information Is Stored

EasyAF stores app data locally on your device using on-device storage technologies. We do not provide user accounts, remote databases, or cloud sync for this app.

Because the App is designed to store data locally, your information generally remains on your device unless you choose to export, share, or send it.

4. Permissions the App Uses

EasyAF may request access to the following device features:

  • Camera: to let you take photos and attach them to documents
  • Photos / Media Library: to let you select existing images and attach them to documents, and to save captured images when you choose to do so

These permissions are used only to support the features you actively use inside the App.

EasyAF does not request or use microphone access.

5. Sharing and Exporting

EasyAF can generate documents such as PDFs and lets you share, print, or email them using apps and services available on your device.

If you choose to share, export, print, or email a document, the selected file may be transferred to third-party apps or services you choose to use, such as your email provider, printer software, or file-sharing apps. Those third parties operate under their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their handling of information once you choose to send or share it through them.

6. Data Sent to Us

We do not receive, collect, or store your project records, documents, photos, or signatures on our own servers through EasyAF because the App does not use a backend service for user data storage.

We do not sell your personal information.

7. Third-Party Services

EasyAF may rely on operating system features and third-party platform components to enable functions such as email composition, file sharing, printing, image selection, and media access on your device.

These components are used only to provide app functionality. If you use third-party apps or services through sharing, printing, or email features, their privacy practices will apply to any data you send through them.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Because EasyAF stores data locally on your device, your information remains available until you delete it within the App, remove the App, or clear the App's local data through your device settings.

If you uninstall the App, locally stored app data may be removed from your device, subject to your device's operating system behavior and any backups you have enabled.

9. Security

We take reasonable steps to design the App so that your information is stored locally on your device rather than transmitted to our servers. However, no device or software environment can be guaranteed to be completely secure. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device, including any screen lock, device encryption, and backup settings you choose to use.

10. Children's Privacy

EasyAF is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through a backend service.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Effective date" at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after an updated policy is posted means the updated policy will apply going forward.
